FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I use Lyyeto®?

Take 5 ml (about half a cap) once daily before your main meal. You can drink it straight or mix it with a little water. No complicated routines needed.

How long does one bottle last?

Each bottle contains 30 ml — that’s about 6 days’ supply at 5 ml per day.

For best results, we recommend a 5-bottle pack (≈30 days) for a full month’s use.

When will I see results?

Many users notice visible changes within 7 days — such as fewer cravings, less bloating, and early weight or centimetre reductions.


For optimal results, use consistently for 3–6 weeks.

Can both men and women use Lyyeto®?

Absolutely. Lyyeto® is suitable for adults of all genders and body types who want natural support for metabolism and healthy weight balance.

Do I need to diet or exercise strictly while using Lyyeto®?

No strict plans required. Lyyeto® is designed to work without extreme dieting or workouts.

Light daily activity and balanced meals can further enhance your results.

Is Lyyeto® safe to use?

Lyyeto® Oral Liquid is formulated with natural, well-known ingredients, including Green Tea Extract, Apple Cider Vinegar Powder, Moringa Leaf Extract, Probiotics, and Turmeric


It contains no harsh stimulants and is produced under Irish and EU safety standards.

Are there any side effects?

Lyyeto® is generally well-tolerated.

If you are pregnant, breastfeeding, or taking medication, please consult your GP or healthcare professional before use.
